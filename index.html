Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and procedures.

Digital X-Ray

Is Digital X-Ray safe?

+
Yes, our digital X-Ray machines use extremely low radiation and are completely safe.

How fast will I get the report?

+
Most X-Ray reports are delivered digitally within 10–15 minutes.

Special Procedures

Are HSG, IVP, MCU, RGU procedures painful?

+
These procedures may cause mild discomfort but are generally safe and performed by expert doctors under continuous monitoring.

Do I need preparation before HSG or IVP?

+
Yes, some procedures require fasting or medications. Our staff will guide you with proper, detailed instructions upon booking.

Mobile & Orthopedic X-Ray

Can X-Ray detect fractures?

+
Yes, orthopedic X-Rays are highly accurate for detecting fractures, sprains, and other bone injuries.

Is Home X-Ray quality good?

+
Yes—our portable digital machines offer the exact same high-resolution image quality as our in-center X-Rays.

General FAQs

Do I need appointment for all tests?

+
  • **Walk-in:** X-Ray, ECG
  • **Appointment Required:** Sonography, 2D Echo, Special Procedures

Can I get reports on WhatsApp?

+
Yes, we send reports via WhatsApp, Email, and provide printed copies as per your preference.

Billing & Reports

Do you accept digital payments?

+
Yes, we accept all major digital payment methods including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe), Debit, and Credit Cards.

Are extra report copies available?

+
Yes, we provide extra printed copies and electronic formats (PDF, WhatsApp) upon request.
